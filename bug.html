Found a game bug?
Submit a bug report and get your name in the game credits*!

Email your bug to charlie@crookspeak.com

Please include as much detail as you can, including:
  • What VR headset are you using?
  • Steam VR or Oculus Store?
  • How would you describe the bug?
  • What level of the game are you on?
  • Has this happened once or multiple times?

    • Anything else i.e. screenshots or video that might help are greatly appreciated.
    *If the bug isn't on the reported bug list I'll add you as a bug hunter to the game's credits!
